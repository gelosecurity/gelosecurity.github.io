Dark Pool Alerts

An autonomous ecosystem that tracks, filters, and routes institutional dark pool blocks and options sweeps into Discord.

This repository evaluates live market data against technical (Minervini) and fundamental (SEPA) constraints to identify Stage 2 breakouts.

🎯 Trading Strategy & Philosophy

This ecosystem automates a specific trading philosophy focused on finding Confluence.

1. Institutional Dark Pools (The Footprints)

Institutions move the market via massive block trades executed off-exchange (Dark Pools). This system tracks trades upwards of $50M to $5B+ to follow the "smart money."

2. Minervini Trend Template (The Technicals)

We target stocks in confirmed Stage 2 Uptrends. The Minervini 8/8 filter helps identify stocks that are technically strong, outperforming the S&P 500, and structurally ready for a breakout.

3. SEPA Growth Engine (The Catalyst)

SEPA (Specific Entry Point Analysis) verifies the fundamental catalysts driving the chart. A stock must have massive Q/Q Earnings growth (>20%), strong Top-Line Sales growth (>20%), and heavy mutual fund backing (>30%) in a top-performing market sector to qualify.

4. "Blue Skies" Absence of Supply (The Resistance)

Historical dark pool blocks act as overhead supply (resistance). We exclusively hunt for stocks trading Above all historical Dark Pool Levels (Blue Skies), meaning there is zero institutional resistance above the current price.

Confluence: When an institution accumulates a massive block of a stock that is structurally robust (Minervini >= 7/8), possesses proven fundamental catalysts (SEPA >= 2/4), and is trading in Blue Skies (Above DP 1/1)—that is a Confluence Strike.

(Note: We specifically use >= 7 and >= 2 to catch explosive turnaround plays that have massive EPS growth but may lack top-line sales or deep institutional ownership yet).

🚨 Webhook Routing Logic (The Alpha Filter)

The bots utilize a multi-routing pipeline to organize alerts:

  • The Record Breakers Webhook (Dark Gold): When the live_whales.py bot detects an institutional print whose dollar volume shatters the 5-year All-Time High, it fires a "Record Breaker" alert. It does not need to be above all DPs to trigger.
  • The "Blue Skies Confluence" Webhook (Discord Blue): A dedicated webhook for high-confluence setups. When an intraday Whale Level Strike OR an All-Time Record Breaker hits the tape and the stock meets strict Confluence (Minervini >= 7/8, SEPA >= 2/4, Above DP == 1/1), it triggers a high-probability alert. The alert turns Discord Blue, is dynamically titled ☁️ BLUE SKIES - RECORD BREAKER or ☁️ BLUE SKIES - WHALE STRIKE, and is diverted exclusively to the Alpha Discord channel.
  • Standard Channels: Normal Dark Pool sweeps and Options flow that pass minimum metrics route to their standard feeds.

🤖 The Bots

1. Intraday Whales (live_whales.py)

Runs continuously intraday. Scans the tape for blocks >$50M. If the block shatters the all-time largest notional print recorded in the database, it triggers a massive "ALL TIME RECORD BREAKER" alert and permanently updates the database. Will dynamically upgrade to ☁️ BLUE SKIES - RECORD BREAKER if it meets Confluence.

2. Whale Level Striker (level_striker.py)

Runs every 5 minutes intraday. Scrapes the TradingView live tape and compares the live price of thousands of stocks to their historical Whale Levels. If a stock enters within ±1.0% of the institutional defense line, it triggers a Strike Alert. If it meets Confluence, it triggers the ☁️ BLUE SKIES - WHALE STRIKE alert.

3. EOD Dark Pool Leaderboard (eod_leaderboard.py)

Runs at market close. Ranks the top 7 dark pool blocks of the day mathematically by their Premium % of Total Market Cap, exposing stealth accumulation in mid/small caps rather than just flat dollar amounts on mega caps. Stacked as isolated Discord Embeds to prevent ReDos markdown failures.

4. Options Bullish Sweeps (options_leaderboard.py)

Runs at market close. Filters millions of options contracts strictly for Ask-Side Sweeps (Aggressive buying taking market liquidity) and Volume > Open Interest (Proving new institutional positions are opening).

🚀 Execution & Deployment

The entire ecosystem is orchestrated serverlessly in the cloud using GitHub Actions (.github/workflows/bots.yml). No local server is required. The Intraday bots run on a chron schedule while the market is open, and the EOD bots run once daily after the closing bell. A programmatic health_check.py runs at 8:45 AM daily to ensure TradingView, Blackboxstocks, Finviz, and the GitHub CI/CD environment are fully online.
