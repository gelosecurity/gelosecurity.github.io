# Dark Pool Alerts An autonomous ecosystem that tracks, filters, and routes institutional dark pool blocks and options sweeps into Discord. This repository evaluates live market data against technical (Minervini) and fundamental (SEPA) constraints to identify Stage 2 breakouts. --- ## 🎯 Trading Strategy & Philosophy This ecosystem automates a specific trading philosophy focused on finding **Confluence**. ### The Core Pillars: 1. **Institutional Dark Pools (The Footprints):** Institutions move the market via massive block trades executed off-exchange (Dark Pools). This system tracks trades upwards of **$50M to $5B+** to follow the "smart money." 2. **Minervini Trend Template (The Technicals):** We only buy stocks in confirmed **Stage 2 Uptrends**. The Minervini 8/8 filter helps identify stocks that are technically strong, outperforming the S&P 500, and structurally ready for a breakout. 3. **SEPA Growth Engine (The Catalyst):** **SEPA (Specific Entry Point Analysis)** verifies the fundamental catalysts driving the chart. A stock must have massive Q/Q Earnings growth (>20%), strong Top-Line Sales growth (>20%), and heavy mutual fund backing (>30%) in a top-performing market sector to qualify. 4. **"Blue Skies" Absence of Supply (The Resistance):** Historical dark pool blocks act as overhead supply (resistance). We exclusively hunt for stocks trading **Above all historical Dark Pool Levels** (Blue Skies), meaning there is zero institutional resistance above the current price. **Confluence:** When an institution accumulates a massive block of a stock that is structurally robust (Minervini >= 7/8), possesses proven fundamental catalysts (SEPA >= 2/4), and is trading in Blue Skies (Above DP 1/1)—that is a **Confluence Strike**. *(Note: We use >= 7 and >= 2 to catch explosive turnaround plays like $NOK that have massive EPS growth but may lack top-line sales or deep institutional ownership yet).* --- ## 🏗️ Architecture & Directory Structure The project has been architected into clean, segregated environments: ``` dark-pool-bot/ ├── core/ # The algorithmic scoring engines │ ├── minervini.py # 8/8 Technical Trend Template engine │ └── sepa.py # 4/4 Fundamental Growth engine ├── data/ # JSON state memory │ ├── db.json # Persistent DB of the all-time largest and highest Dark Pool blocks per ticker │ └── daily_alerts.json # Temporary memory to prevent Discord webhook spam ├── discord_bots/ # Isolated routing and alert bots │ ├── darkpool_intraday/ # Intraday Record Breaker (live_whales.py) │ ├── darkpool_levels/ # Intraday Whale Defense levels (level_striker.py) │ ├── darkpool_eod/ # End-of-Day Top 7 Market Cap blocks (eod_leaderboard.py) │ └── options_sweeps/ # End-of-Day Bullish Vol > OI Sweeps (options_leaderboard.py) ├── extension/ # Chrome Extension for PineScript generation (auto-adjusts for stock splits) └── .github/workflows/bots.yml # CI/CD Cloud orchestrator ``` --- ## 🧠 The Scoring Engines Every single trade is evaluated through three separate algorithmic engines to define **Confluence**. ### 1. Minervini Trend Template (8/8) Calculates the exact structural health of a stock chart. A stock must score at least **7/8** to pass standard filters. 1. Stock Price > 150 & 200 SMA 2. 150 SMA > 200 SMA 3. 200 SMA is trending up for >1 month 4. 50 SMA > 150 & 200 SMA 5. Stock Price > 50 SMA 6. Stock Price > 52-Week Low + 25% 7. Stock Price is within 25% of 52-Week High 8. **Relative Strength (RS) Rating > 70** *(Using exact weighted 3, 6, 9, 12-month PineScript algorithm).* ### 2. SEPA Fundamental Growth Engine (4/4) Silently scrapes Finviz to verify the "Rocket Fuel" (catalyst). A stock must score at least **2/4** to meet Confluence. It extracts Raw EPS, Sales Growth, Institutional Ownership %, Short Float %, and the Next Earnings Date. 1. **EPS Growth:** Q/Q Earnings Per Share >= 20% 2. **Sales Growth:** Q/Q Top-Line Revenue >= 20% 3. **Institutional Ownership:** Smart money backing >= 30% 4. **Sector Momentum:** The stock currently belongs to one of the Top 4 performing market sectors (dynamically calculated live against SPDR ETFs). ### 3. Above Dark Pool Resistance (1/1) A boolean constraint that strictly defines "Blue Skies" by checking the live TradingView price against the `data/db.json` database. * It finds the **Ceiling Price** (the absolute highest price any >$2M institutional Dark Pool block was ever printed at for that ticker). * If Live Price > The Ceiling Price: Score is **1/1**. * This proves there is **Zero Institutional Supply Overhead**. --- ## 🚨 Webhook Routing Logic (The Alpha Filter) The bots utilize a multi-routing pipeline to organize alerts: * **The Record Breakers Webhook (Dark Gold):** When the `live_whales.py` bot detects an institutional print whose dollar volume shatters the 5-year All-Time High, it fires a "Record Breaker" alert. It does **not** need to be above all DPs to trigger. * **The "Blue Skies Confluence" Webhook (Discord Blue):** A dedicated webhook for high-confluence setups. When an intraday **Whale Level Strike** OR an **All-Time Record Breaker** hits the tape and the stock meets strict **Confluence** (`Minervini >= 7/8`, `SEPA >= 2/4`, `Above DP == 1/1`), it triggers a high-probability alert. The alert turns Discord Blue, is dynamically titled `☁️ BLUE SKIES - RECORD BREAKER` or `☁️ BLUE SKIES - WHALE STRIKE`, and is diverted exclusively to the Alpha Discord channel. * **Standard Channels:** Normal Dark Pool sweeps and Options flow that pass minimum metrics route to their standard feeds. --- ## 📊 Visual UI Output All bots utilize heavily formatted, native Discord ANSI code blocks for dark-mode readability: ```ansi Value : $500.00M Price : $155.00 Previous: $350.00M Prev Px : $130.00 ... (or for Strikes) ... Gold DP : $144.71 (Heaviest Block) Ceiling : $150.00 (Highest Price) Live : $155.00 Strength: $479.7M (Printed: 1/30/2026) Inst Own: 45.94% | Short: 22.13% 🚨 Growth : EPS +96.65% | Sales +73.21% Earnings: May 20 AMC Rating : Minervini 8/8 • SEPA 4/4 • Above DP 1/1 ``` *(Notice the split between Gold DP and Ceiling to perfectly visually map the Above DP logic, and the Previous Px tracking on Record Breakers).* *(The `🚨` emoji dynamically triggers if Short Interest exceeds 15%, warning of a massive short-squeeze potential).* --- ## 🤖 The Bots ### 1. Intraday Whales (`live_whales.py`) * Runs continuously intraday. * Scans the tape for blocks >$50M. If the block shatters the all-time largest notional print recorded in `db.json`, it triggers a massive "ALL TIME RECORD BREAKER" alert and permanently updates the database. Will dynamically upgrade to `☁️ BLUE SKIES - RECORD BREAKER` if it meets Confluence. ### 2. Whale Level Striker (`level_striker.py`) * Runs every 5 minutes intraday. * Scrapes the TradingView live tape and compares the live price of thousands of stocks to their historical Whale Levels. * If a stock enters within ±1.0% of the institutional defense line, it triggers a Strike Alert. If it meets Confluence, it triggers the `☁️ BLUE SKIES - WHALE STRIKE` alert. ### 3. EOD Dark Pool Leaderboard (`eod_leaderboard.py`) * Runs at market close. * Ranks the top 7 dark pool blocks of the day mathematically by their **Premium % of Total Market Cap**, exposing stealth accumulation in mid/small caps. Stacked as isolated Discord Embeds to prevent ReDos markdown failures. ### 4. Options Bullish Sweeps (`options_leaderboard.py`) * Runs at market close. * Filters millions of options contracts strictly for: * **Ask-Side Sweeps** (Aggressive buying taking market liquidity). * **Volume > Open Interest** (Proving new institutional positions are opening, rather than old ones closing). * Evaluates Days to Expiration (DTE) and Moneyness (ITM/OTM/ATM). --- ## 🚀 Execution & Deployment The entire ecosystem is orchestrated serverlessly in the cloud using **GitHub Actions** (`.github/workflows/bots.yml`). No local server is required. * The Intraday bots run on a chron schedule while the market is open. * The EOD bots run once daily after the closing bell. * A programmatic `health_check.py` runs at 8:45 AM daily to ensure TradingView, Blackboxstocks, Finviz, and the GitHub CI/CD environment are fully online. # Dark Pool Alerts An autonomous ecosystem that tracks, filters, and routes institutional dark pool blocks and options sweeps into Discord.
